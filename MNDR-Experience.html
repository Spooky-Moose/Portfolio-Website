MNDR VR Experience

Project information

  • Category: VR Development
  • Category: Lead Developer & Technical UX Designer
  • Engine: Unity VR
  • Client: MNDR
  • Project Timeline: October 2022 - May 2023
  • Project URL: mndr-experience

This VR Experience was created for singer, songwriter and producer, MNDR. We were tasked to create an immersive VR experience of Amanda Warner's music and themes. I helped lead a full development team in building this project while also coordinating with an Art and Design team for asset creation. Many of my contributions included managing version control, level design, timeline management, and client fulfillment. This project is being passed onto a new team who will be developing it further to be showcased at Treefort Music Fest and South By Southwest

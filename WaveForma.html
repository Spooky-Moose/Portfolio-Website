WaveForma

Project information

  • Category: Interactive Development
  • Role: Lead Developer & Designer
  • Engine: Unity
  • Client: The Keith and Catherine Stein Luminary
  • Project Timeline: October 2022 - Present

WaveForma was created as part of a client project during my senior courses in GIMM. A classmate and I were tasked with updating an Audiovisual Experience that had previously been worked on to be showcased and used in the Stein Luminary at Boise State Campus. Our visualizer was showcased during Treefort Music Fest alongside a handful of other interactive experiences.
